Andrew Burchill is currently working on his Ph.D. in Collective Animal Behavior with Arizona State University's Social Insect Research Group.

My 'Thang':

Ants. Anything with ants. Evolution, physiology, but especially behavior. Also things that aren't ants but remind me of ants? This includes swarm dynamics, decentralized optimization problems, and optimal foraging theory.

My research right now is focused on how ant colonies do family meal-planning. Just a few workers do the "grocery shopping" for the entire 200+ member colony: how do they coordinate their foraging without language or any central authority?
