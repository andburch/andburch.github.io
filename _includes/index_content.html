Andrew Burchill is currently working on his doctoral thesis in Collective Animal Behavior with Arizona State University's Social Insect Research Group.

My Jam:

My research right now is focused on how ant colonies do family meal-planning. Just a few workers do the "grocery shopping" for the entire 200+ member colony: how do they coordinate their foraging without language or any central authority?
