Why is your logo a weird-looking fruit-thing?

It's a pawpaw, the largest edible fruit indigenous to North America. It's native to where I grew up (Indiana), and a well-kept delicious secret even to locals. Imagine a cross between banana custard and a mango--pawpaws are absolutely amazing!

More importantly, it's a holdover from when I forked a branch of my twin brother's website! I haven't gotten around to changing it yet... Or even adding my own content.
